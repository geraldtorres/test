Photographer & Filmmaker

My name is Brent Torres, and I'd like to introduce myself. I like to express myself through pictures and films. In 2020, during the pandemic, I started a YouTube channel to document my surfing progress in short montages. Over the next year, I began to venture out and share experiences from my life from my perspective (POV). After my first year and a half, I've created a business out of my storytelling.

Now I upload major projects on YouTube, ranging from activities with friends, vlogs, travel videos, and short films. I also create for businesses as my part-time job.
